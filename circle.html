Circle by Providence

Freelance Graphic Design

Overview

Providence Health and Swedish Medical Center, two west coast not-for-profit networks of hospitals, care centers, and health plans, recently released new maternity apps in partnership with Circle Women’s Health Platform full of resources and tools for moms and moms-to-be about raising healthy, happy children. The Circle teams at Providence and Swedish needed professional email templates created to ensure that the digital communications they were sending to users were on-brand and reflected the aesthetic of their respective apps. I was asked to design and build these templates within their email campaign management systems.

Problem

The email communications that the Circle teams at Providence Health and Swedish Medical Centers were sending out were basic and not consistent with the non-profits’ branding and aesthetic. They were also starting from scratch building each email they were sending out instead of relying on predesigned templates to work from.

Solution

I designed and built six email templates each for Providence Health, Swedish Medical, and Circle within their email campaign management systems, relying on each brand’s style guide for color and layout direction. The range of designs provided the teams flexibility in their email marketing to include photos and a CTA if desired.

Technologies Used

  • Sketch
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • MailChimp

Template Breakdown

Content Requirements

Based off of conversations with my client I came with a list of requirements for the project:

  • Each brand — Providence Health, Swedish Medical, Circle — needed six templates: one template with no photos, one template with one photo, one template with two photos; each of these needed a version with a CTA button and a version without

  • Each template needed to incorporate the brand’s colors and provided logos

  • Each template needed a link to the corresponding brand’s app in the iOS App Store and the Google Play Store

  • Each template needed a footer containing subscription management options, an email view option, a copyright mark, and mailing address information to comply with CAN-SPAN laws

Wireframe

UI

I made sure to prescribe to each brand’s colors to ensure consistency for users between their app experience and the email messages they would receive on behalf of each app’s communication teams. I was also provided logo assets to build into my design that incorporate the Circle logo with those of Providence Health and Swedish Medical.
